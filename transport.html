Airport Bus - Vayu Vajra



The BMTC Vayu Vajra AC bus service is an efficient and inexpensive mode of transport. The fleet of air-conditioned buses offers seamless service to and from the City.

Information: Students arriving from Ukraine in Bengaluru can avail free rides to their destination by KSRTC bus service at Kempegowda International Airport Bengaluru.

For queries, please contact KSRTC call centre on 080-26252625.

Note: Mode of payment >> Bus fare will be collected by cash only, upon boarding.
